Finnish College Gamejam 2022

Gamejam between vocational schools

The theme was "start with nothing". Me and my group made a 2D platformer where the player gains their powers along the way from the dots they collect. Therefore, starts with nothing. The player can also lose powers from the collectible dots. The idea is pretty simple, speedrun game. I focused mostly on the level designs. I made them with a tile palette. I also made the main menu of the game, and the level select panel.
